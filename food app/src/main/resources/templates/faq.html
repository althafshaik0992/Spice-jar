Frequently Asked Questions

Where do you source your spices?

We work directly with sustainable farms and cooperatives around the world to ensure the highest quality and freshness. We're committed to fair trade practices and ethical sourcing.

Are your spices organic?

Many of our spices are certified organic. You can check each product's description for specific details on its sourcing and certification.

What is your shipping policy?

We offer standard shipping (3-5 business days) and expedited shipping (1-2 business days). Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your order total and location.

Do you accept returns?

Yes, we accept returns on unopened and unused products within 30 days of purchase. Please contact our support team to initiate a return request.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach us through the "Contact Us" form on our website, or by emailing us at support@thespicejar.com. We typically respond within 24-48 hours.
